Latavius Rashard Murray is an American football running back for the Denver Broncos of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at UCF and was drafted by the Oakland Raiders in the sixth round of the 2013 NFL Draft. He was also a member of the Minnesota Vikings, New Orleans Saints, and Baltimore Ravens.

Murray was not invited to the NFL Combine but garnered much attention with an impressive performance at Central Florida’s Pro Day.

Murray weighed in at 6-foot-2 5/8 and 223 pounds, ran the 40-yard dash in 4.4 and 4.38 seconds, registered a 10-foot-4 broad jump and a 36-inch vertical jump, ran 4.36 seconds in the short shuttle and 6.81 seconds in the 3-cone.

Murray was signed to the Saints practice squad on September 13, 2022. He was elevated to the active roster on October 1, 2022. Denver Broncos. Murray was signed off the Saints practice squad by the Denver Broncos following a season-ending injury to Javonte Williams.
