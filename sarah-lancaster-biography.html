Who is Sarah Lancaster?

Sarah Lancaster is a famous American actress. She is popular for her role as Rachel Meyers in the NBC series ‘Saved by the Bell: The New Class’. She is also well recognized as Ellie Bartowski in the NBC comedy-spy series ‘Chuck’ and as Mariorie in the ABC’s TV series ‘What About Brian’.

How old is Sarah Lancaster?

She was born on February 12, 1980, in Overland Park, Kansas, USA. She is currently 42 years old. Her father’s name is Michael(real estate developer) and her mother’s name is Barbara(homemaker).

She has got a brother named Daniel and she grew up alongside her brother. His father got a job in Mission Viejo when she was 10 years old and the family had to shift to Mission Viejo, California.

Education, School/College University

Talking about her education, Sarah Lancaster completed her education in California. During high school, she developed her interest in acting and took acting lessons at California’s Actors Workshop. Later, she enrolled at the University of California and graduated there.

Sarah Lancaster: Professional Life and Career

She debuted her acting career on the television screen when she portrayed the role of Rachel Meyers in the series ‘Saved by the Bell: The New Class’ in 1993. She made her appearance in a couple of TV series in 1997 like ‘Sabrina’, ‘Teenage Witch and Night Man’, ‘Unhappily Ever After’, etc.

Sarah played the leading role of Lia in an American anthology series ‘Undresses’ in 2003 in which she portrayed the role of Madison Kellner in 20 episodes of the series. She appeared in a lead role in an American drama television series ‘What About Brian’.

The talented actress debuted her film career with the movie ‘Lovers lane’ in 1999. Then, she made her appearance in the animated musical comedy film ‘Teacher’s Pet’ in 2000 and also made her presence in the American thriller film ‘The Good Doctor’ in 2011.

Sarah Lancaster was featured in the American drama film ‘The Judge’ alongside Robert Downey Jr. in 2014. Recently, she portrayed the role of Linda in the film ‘The Terror of Hallow’s Eve’ in 2017 and Sarah in ‘Christmas on Holly Lane’ in 2018.

Awards, Nominations

Sarah Lancaster was nominated for Young Artist Award in the category Best Performance in a TV series-Young Ensemble for ‘Saved by the Bell: The New Class’ in 1997, nominated for Young Artist Award in the category Best Performance by a Young Actress-TV Comedy Series for ‘Saved by the Bell’ in 1996 and nominated for YoungStar Award for Best Performance by a Young Actress in a Saturday Morning TV Program for ‘Saved by the Bell’ in 1997.
