Pamela Sue Martin is an American actress and a former model who has an estimated net worth of $4 million.

Martin began modeling at age 17. Her first appearance was in the film The Poseidon Adventure.

She landed the role of Fallon Carrington Colby on the ABC soap opera, Dynasty, in 1981. She appeared in several films and TV series, such as The Girls of Huntington House, The Gun and the Pulpit, Buster and Billie, Our Time, Human Feelings, The Lady in Red, Torchlight, Strong Medicine, Bay Coven, Flicks, and The Saint: The Software Murders, among others. She landed the role of Nancy Drew in The Hardy Boys/Nancy Drew Mysteries that was widely recognized.

Pamela Sue Martin was born on January 5, 1953 in Hartford, Connecticut, USA. She was divorced three times and a mother of one son. She manages her own theatre company in Idaho.
