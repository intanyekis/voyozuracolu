Nicki Minaj, Drake &amp; Lil Wayne Drop &quot;No Frauds&quot; Video

August 01, 2024

Nicki Minaj takes her self-appointed role as the “Queen of Rap” to a whole other level in the new video for “No Frauds” featuring Drake and Lil Wayne, which finds her heading to London and dressing up in garb fit for a royal (albeit much more risqué).

The song is taken from the #3PackFromParis she delivered early last month and is considered a mild response to Remy Ma’s diss track “ShETHER.”

With lines like, “You can’t be Pablo if your work ain’t sellin’/What the fuck is this bitch inhalin’?…”Back to back, oh you mean, back to wack? ‘Back to Back?’ Me and Drizzy laughed at that/They say numbers don’t matter but when they discussin’ the kings/They turn around and say Lebron ain’t got six rings/I never signed a 360, bitch you wild dumb,” she made it clear she was after Remy’s jugular.
