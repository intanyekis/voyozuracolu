SCREAM 6 SPOILERS: Who Is The Real Ghostface Revealed In Leaked Clip?

July 28, 2024
blog

The anticipation for Scream 6 is at an all-time high as fans eagerly await to find out who the killer is. It’s been nearly 25 years since Woodsboro was first introduced in Wes Craven’s original, and it continues to be a thrilling journey ever since.

Back in the director’s seat are Matt Bettinelli-Olpin and Tyler Gillett, with Courteney Cox and Jenna Ortega reprising their roles. In addition, Hayden Panettiere returns as Kirby from the fourth installment.

In the new movie, our characters find themselves in New York City and facing a new killer – one that we got a sneak peek of with a trailer full of easter eggs.

Recently there were rumors that Scream 6’s ghostface had been leaked online – revealing what could be key plot points of the movie. 

The leak ghostface

A recently emerged on Twitter showing the alleged ending of Scream 6.

The scene, in particular, contains the moments when the killer is revealed, Game Revolution reports.

Since then, the clip has been removed from Twitter, with Paramount Pictures and Spyglass Media Group ensuring it’s offline. Nevertheless, it’s already been seen by a bunch of fans.

The audiences were surprised to stumble across the clip under the Scream 6 hashtag and have warned others to stay off the platform to avoid unearthing major spoilers potentially.

While the clip definitely looks like it’s from the 2023 sequel – the actors are there etc. – some fans are arguing that it may be a fake killer reveal to throw off viewers. 

A fabricated spoiler, if you will, as a fake script for Scream 2 was leaked when that was released, designed to surprise audiences when they released they hadn’t seen the actual script.

Go ahead for those who want to know who Ghostface is in the Scream 6 leak. 

If you don’t want to know, then you’ve been warned. Potential and major spoilers ahead.

Did the leak reveal the killer?

Rumors have been swirling around the alleged ending of Scream 6, and now, it’s been revealed that there are two killers – Detective Bailey (Dermot Mulroney) and his accomplice Ethan (Jack Champion). 

The leak suggests that they’re related to Stu Macher (Matthew Lillard), one of the two killers from the original movie – giving them a solid motivation to take out any potential victims.

While this might be true, we can’t confirm if this is actually the official ending for Scream 6.

It could very well be an alternative ending that was leaked, so for now, audiences should remain skeptical and keep their minds open until the movie hits theaters in March 2023.
