Jack Ciattarelli is an American politician and businessman who served as a nominee for governor in the 2021 New Jersey gubernatorial election. Prior to this, Jack Ciattarelli previously served in the New Jersey General Assembly from 2011 to 2018.

Jack Ciattarelli won the Republican gubernatorial nominee on June 8, 2021, and contested in the election with Phil Murphy on November 2, 2021.
