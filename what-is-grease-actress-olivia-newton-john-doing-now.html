What Is 'Grease' Actress Olivia Newton-John Doing Now?

June 20, 2024
blog

We fell in love with her angel face in the 1978 box office hit, Grease, but what's Olivia Newton-John up to these days? For over four decades now, audiences have been enraptured by the musical that brought the unforgettable Danny Zuko and Sandy Olsson together. And after all these years, we can't get the Sandy that was dressed in an all-black skintight outfit out of our heads - yes, she's still the one we want!

Related: Why is Twitter Not Okay With A 'Grease' Prequel?

Even though we haven't seen Newton-John on the big screen in a while, her iconic role is engrained in our minds. Alas, the Australian Grammy winner has been way busier than we think. If you're hopelessly devoted to Newton-John, you can find out all she's been up to in recent years below.

10 The C Word

At 72, the beloved Grease star is going through her third cancer treatment.

In 2017, Newton-John received the terrible news that she was diagnosed with stage 4 metastatic breast cancer. The news was even more tragic as it's her third bout with cancer. According to Closer Weekly, she was first diagnosed with breast cancer in 1992, then in 2013, and for decades now she has tried to treat the disease with various methods, including chemotherapy and radiation.

9 She's A Fighter

Living with cancer isn't easy, but Newton-John is a trooper! The unstoppable star has been fighting and thriving for over two decades now, despite her latest stage 4 diagnosis.

Newton-John has been nothing short of inspiring. She has kept a positive attitude throughout, and in a recent interview on The One Show, she pointed out that she's "feeling wonderful." Not even cancer can stop the "Hopelessly Devoted to You" singer from being optimistic. She said of her stage 4 cancer, "Oh no, I'm fine, I just – listen, I was dealing with metastatic breast cancer for the last seven years but I feel great."

8 Cancer Charity

She's unstoppable!

Newton-John has taken a bad situation and turned it into something good. A cancer patient herself, ONJ founded the Olivia Newton-John Foundation late last year to fund research for treatments and therapies to cure cancer. Having gone through so many struggles with the different types of treatments, the icon told Good Morning America why she decided to launch a foundation.

"I've always thought, 'Gosh, wouldn't it be wonderful if we could create kinder therapies that help boost the body's immune system instead of knocking us down?'" she said.

Her ongoing efforts are incredible!

7 Mother-Daughter Time

The pandemic forced many families to spend a lot more time together, while others were torn apart.

Newton-John and her daughter Chloe Lattanzi, who she had from her marriage to her ex-husband, have always been inseparable. The duo share a tight bond, however, when the quarantine came around, they became much closer as they lived through it together in California.

The busy mother saw the quarantine as a blessing in disguise for herself and her daughter. She told People, "I worked my whole life, and the longest period I can remember being home was my pregnancy with Chloe and the first year or two of her life, so it's been wonderful reconnecting with my baby. She is my reason to be." So precious!

6 A Day In The Life Of ONJ

What does a typical day look like for the mother-daughter duo?

The talented ladies really take advantage of every hour of every day when they're together. On Newton-John's picturesque California ranch, one can find beautiful miniature horses, and both Newton-John and Lattanzi showing them love. For Newton-John, serenity is being out in nature and hearing the birds chirp. Spending time in the outdoors alongside her daughter has been her healing force for the last year and a half.

5 A Duet

Besides enjoying their quiet space together away from the city, Newton-John and Lattanzi went back in the studio - together.

It wasn't their first shot at a mother-daughter duet as they had previously recorded "You Have to Believe." Time passed, and with the trying times the world has been going through collectively, and Newton-John's battle, they thought the timing was perfect to release a healing soundtrack. Titled "The Window in the Wall," the ballad is deep, but enlightening. When Newton-John reviewed the lyrics during the pandemic, she knew she had to sing it with her daughter.

"I knew immediately that I wanted to sing it with my daughter Chloe," Newton-John said in a press release.

4 An Eternal Friendship

It's been over four decades, but Sandy hasn't forgotten her Danny Zuko that she met at the beach!

Although John Travolta and Newton-John made a spicy onscreen couple, in real life, they weren't an item. However, all these years have passed, and the two remain incredible friends. Her co-star in the musical became a lifelong friend, one that she holds close to her heart. She told ET Canada, "[John]’s a dear friend and he always will be and you know Didi, also."

The selfless star was also by Travolta's side when he lost his wife, Kelly Preston, to cancer.

3 Plant-Based

Newton-John has recently opted for a healthier lifestyle by alternating her diet.

Switching to a plant-based diet is just one of her newest approaches to battling cancer. And after having spent the most part of last year and this year with her daughter, who's vegan, it aided in making the transition smoother. Newton-John said, "I've also been eating vegan because my daughter was visiting me and she’s a vegan. I feel very good."

2 Holistic Healing

And that's not at all! Newton-John has been doing all she could to improve her health following her third diagnosis, including a switch to medicinal cannabis.

The English-Australian actress, with the support of her husband, John Easterling, has been using medical cannabis and herbal remedies in her fight against stage 4 cancer. And, coincidentally enough, her partner happens to be a plant medicine man and an expert in cannabis. Newton-John said of her husband to People, "Now he's growing medicinal cannabis for me, and it just has been wonderful. It helps me in every area." And she's feeling superb!

1 A Family Business

While Newton-John is dealing with her ongoing cancer battle, the sweet star is still thinking about the health of others.

By focusing on plant-based medicine, Newton-John, her husband and Lattanzi have collaboratively commenced a cannabis business. When the duo had an interview with The Hollywood Reporter, Lattanzi answered a few questions about their newest venture. She said,  "There are two. It’s Bio Harmonic Tonic, which is a microbial product to help your garden and your plants flourish. And we have our farm, Laughing Dog Farms. We do everything organically."Oh, and they use their beautiful voices to sing to their plants!
