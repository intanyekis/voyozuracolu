J. Cole Emailed Fans Dreamville's 'Revenge Of The Dreamers II' Project

June 08, 2024
blog

The Dreamville collective surprised fans late Monday evening (Dec. 7) with the release of their Revenge Of The Dreamers II. But for a few blessed fans, they received the 9-track project way before it hit iTunes for the masses.

Taking to Twitter to share their delight, a few followers of J. Cole announced that the Fayetteville native slid in their inbox, but to gift them with an early Christmas present. Dreamville’s current soundscape was available for those lucky listeners’ pleasure, thanking Cole for the surprise gesture.

With a simple tweet from the “Love Yours” rapper, who said, “Need somebody email right quick,” followers immediately shared their Gmail, Yahoo, and maybe AOL, accounts.

ROTD2 was originally set for release on Friday (Dec. 11), but according to Dreamville’s co-founder, Ibrahim Hamad, they instead decided to drop it yesterday.

Album was suppose to come out Friday but we moved it up for ya. So to everyone international, Tidal, spotify, google play etc. coming soon

The compilation also features Dreamville’s latest signees Ari Lennox and Lute, who are listed on a song a piece.
