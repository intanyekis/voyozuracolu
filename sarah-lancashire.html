Where was Sarah Lancashire born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Sarah Lancashire was born on October 10, 1964, in Oldham, Lancashire, England, to Geoffrey Lancashire (1933-2004) and Hilda McCormack. Sarah Lancashire was raised by three brothers named Simon, John, and James Lancashire. She is British in nationality and of English and Irish ancestry, so she is of mixed ethnicity. She will be 59 years old in 2023. Between 1976 and 1981, the actress attended Oldham Hulme Grammar School before enrolling at Lancashire’s Guildhall School of Music and Drama.

She graduated in 1986, describing her time at the university as “tremendous,” but also “seriously hard work and quite intimidating.” She did not seriously consider pursuing a career in the performing arts until she was 18 years old. In March 2002, Salford University bestowed upon her an Honorary Master of Arts degree.
