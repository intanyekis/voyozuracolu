Respected Chicago Periodontist, Dr. Amarik Singh, Named One of America's Top Dentists

July 01, 2024
blog

CHICAGO, June 19, 2018 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- In addition to numerous honors he has received, Chicago, IL, periodontist, Dr. Amarik Singh, has been named one of America's Top Dentists by Consumers' Research Council of America. Throughout his time practicing dentistry in the Chicago area, Dr. Singh has placed more than 15,000 dental implants and offers advanced periodontal treatments such as full arch All-on-4® dental implants, the Chao Pinhole® Surgical Technique and laser gum disease treatment.

The Top Dentists list is tabulated based on four specific criteria. Notably, the Consumers' Research Council of America judges dental practitioners on their years of experience, type of board certification or certifications, training beyond what's necessary given their chosen focus, and professional associations and memberships. No payments are exchanged to be added to this list of Top Dentists. Dr. Singh ranked high in each of these subjective categories, marking him as one of the premiere periodontists in the nation.

Dr. Singh has spent his extensive career as a respected periodontist in Chicago, IL, developing a reputation for advanced, customized treatment options for a variety of patient periodontal needs. From traditional periodontal care to precise, high-tech guided dental implant surgeries, he remains at the forefront of his industry and brings leading-edge care to everyone he serves.

Dr. Singh and his team welcome new patients who are interested in learning more about dental implants and other advanced treatments. Referrals are not needed to schedule an initial examination and consultation. Interested persons from anywhere in the country with or without a referral are invited to contact Dr. Singh's office, Periodontal Implant Associates, at 630-424-9404.

About the Periodontist

Periodontal Implant Associates is a periodontal practice offering personalized dental care for patients in Oak Brook, IL and the Chicago area. After graduating from Northwestern University Dental School, Dr. Amarik Singh went on to obtain his Specialty Certificate in Periodontics and earn his Master's from Northwestern University. Dr. Singh is deeply committed to continuing education and prides himself on remaining on the cutting-edge of the newest techniques and technology in dentistry. Dr. Singh has placed over 15,000 dental implants using minimally invasive techniques and is part of an elite group of dental professionals at the forefront of the Chao Pinhole® Surgical Technique (PST™), a revolutionary new approach to repairing gum recession. To learn more about the periodontal services available at Periodontal Implant Associates, please visit http://www.pidentists.com or call 630-424-9404.
