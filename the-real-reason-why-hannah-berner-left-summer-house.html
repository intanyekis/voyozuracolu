The Real Reason Why Hannah Berner Left Summer House

July 27, 2024
blog

Hannah Berner joined the Bravo family after becoming a full-time cast member on Summer House in its third season.

The podcaster and self-proclaimed comic was surely a great fit for the series, considering she brought everything needed to make reality TV gold. Despite her witty ways, Hannah's most recent appearance on season 5 was definitely a rocky season for her.

After countless feuds that left fans divided this season, Hannah was in the hot seat all reunion long! Following her difficult time during the season wrap-up, Hannah revealed she would not be returning to the show, and here's why!

Why Hannah Left 'Summer House'

Hannah Berner first came to prominence when her tweets and memes were picked up by countless social media pages, sparking her start with hit digital media company, Betches.

Following her time as an editor, Hannah transitioned from editorial work to podcasting! Berner started her very own podcast, Berning In Hell, which has become quite a popular show.

Her fame and success only skyrocketed when it was announced that she would be joining the cast of Summer House in its third season. While Hannah was definitely a ticking time bomb throughout the series, it appears as if things came to an explosive end for Berner following season 5.

After enduring one of the rockiest summers yet, Hannah found herself in the hot seat for most of the season, going up to bat against Lindsay Hubbard, Luke Gulbranson, Amanda Batula, Carl Radke, and Kyle Cooke, and that's just in her final season.

Hannah became the main target during the season reunion, leaving fans exhausted over the fact that she could not take accountability for any of her wrongdoings.

Viewers called out Hannah for her hypocrisy, inability to apologize or take responsibility for her actions last summer, which ultimately landed her on the outs with her now, former houseguests.

While her less than stellar season was one reason for Hannah's departure from the show, it seems as if there is far more to her leaving than fans think. According to Entertainment Tonight, Hannah's departure also stemmed from people calling her out on allegedly stealing jokes from other comedians.

The star has also gone onto her podcast and made questionable comments about mental health and many accusations against her former co-stars, including the time she claimed Carl Radke had a drug problem, which he called her out on during the season 5 reunion.

Fans were quick to check Hannah for evolving into a not-so-great individual, despite her personality making great TV.

Luckily for Hannah, she has a great support system in her new soon-to-be husband, Des Bishop. The duo announced their engagement earlier this year after knowing one another for less than a year.

Des made an appearance on Summer House this season, and fans concluded that he and Hannah are practically the same person, hence why things worked out between the two.

Despite her departure from the show, it appears as if we'll be getting out favorites back for season 6, however, till then, fans can mark their calendar for the Summer House and Southern Charm mashup, Summer House: Winter Charmn, which is set to air later this year.
