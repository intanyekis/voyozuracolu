Carl Edwards is an American professional car race driver with an estimated net worth of $46 million.

Carl Edwards pursued his career goals in racing. Before hitting the pit stops, he worked as a substitute teacher. In 2002, he competed in 7 NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series and became a full time Truck Series participant in 2003.

He was a frequent participant at Sprint Cup Series, Daytona 500, Nationwide Series, and Xfinity Series, among others. Edwards was a recipient of several recognitions as a professional racer which include the 2007 NASCAR Busch Series Champion, 2011 NASCAR Sprint All-Star Race winner, 2007 Prelude to the Dream winner, and 2015 Coca-Cola 600 winner. He was recognized as Rookie of the Year during the 2003 NASCAR Busch Series, and Craftsman Truck Series.

Carl Michael Edwards II was born on August 15, 1979 in Columbia, Missouri.
