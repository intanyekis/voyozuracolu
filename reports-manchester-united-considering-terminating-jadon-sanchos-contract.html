Reports: Manchester United considering terminating Jadon Sanchos contract

May 20, 2024
blog

There are reports from Sky Sports suggesting that Manchester United may be considering terminating Jadon Sancho’s contract.

It is believed that this decision is a result of the new cultural and recruitment strategy being implemented under Sir Jim Ratcliffe, the incoming minority owner.

The INEOS group, led by Ratcliffe, aims to foster a more positive working culture and take a more decisive approach in dealing with players who do not align with the club’s ethos.

Manchester United is reportedly inclined to terminate contracts of such players rather than waiting to secure a transfer fee for them.

The club has faced challenges in this area in recent times, notably with the handling of Jadon Sancho’s situation and the extended saga involving Mason Greenwood.

INEOS and Ratcliffe have shown admiration for Arsenal’s approach in revamping their sporting culture by canceling contracts of players like Mesut Ozil, Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang, and Nicolas Pepe in recent years.
