Former 'Love Island' Star Molly Mae Hague Got Paid $550,000 A Month By Pretty Little Thing

The former Love Island reality TV star Molly-Mae Hague is now a multi-millionaire thanks to her seven figure deal with the fashion brand Pretty Little Thing (PLT). It was recently reported that she was making about $550,000 a month as a creative director of the fashion brand. Molly-Mae has announced last year that she just made her biggest career move to date after signing a seven figure deal with PLT in exchange of rendering her service as the creative director of the brand.

With her salary from the fashion brand co-founded by Umar Kamani, and her strings of brand deals from different establishments, she has now become a multi-millionaire. It was also reported that she can earn up to $17,000 daily on her Instagram account just by posting different brands on it. Molly-Mae has come a long way since starring on the reality TV show Love Island with breaking the record as the richest Islander to date. It was reported that it was always been her dream to be creative director for a fashion brand and PLT made her dream become a reality.
