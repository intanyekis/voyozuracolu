Detectives Seize Embakasi West MP George Theuri's Car Manufactured At a Fictitious Mombasa Company

July 15, 2024
blog

A car belonging to Embakasi West member of Parliament George Theuri’s wife Cynthia Gitonga was on Saturday impounded by DCI detectives.

In a tweet, DCI said one of the cars belonged to Ms Gitonga and the other John Mwangi.

The vehicles were declared semi trailers hence evaded duty through an organized scheme that claims they were manufactured at a fictitious company in Mombasa, the DCI noted.

I want the whole world to know that I love you very much and I honor and cherish every moment with you my one and only queen,” he said.
