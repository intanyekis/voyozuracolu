Las Vegas is the epicenter of the American entertainment industry where people go to watch several live shows performed by some of the best in the world. These range from acrobatics to dancing, but the most popular are those that feature live music. Many famous artists perform at these venues for months at a time, and the tickets are something only the wealthy can afford.

These venues are the cream of the crop in terms of acts, food, and service. These establishments aim to please and bring back top spenders time and time again. Keep reading to learn about the 10 best high-end live music venues in Las Vegas!

10. Hard Rock Hotel & Casino's Rehab Pool

This pool is part of the Hard Rock Hotel & Casino, and it is the place to be if you like to party. They hold events every weekend with a variety of live music and contests, as well as on-site casinos, restaurants, full-service salons and spas.

Friday nights come alive as the stage is set for a poolside concert in the summertime. A few of their past performers include Blue October, G. Love, and Rusted Root. You can always choose to relax beside the Nirvana pool the next day to recover from this live music experience.

9. MGM Grand Garden Arena

If you desire to see some of the biggest names in music perform, then you should head to this venue. You can watch artists like Ariana Grande, Cher, Niall Horan, and so many more.

If you want good seats it will cost you an arm and a leg, but the accommodations will seat you in the lap of luxury. You can stay in the hotel and choose from a variety of high-end suites, as well as stop by the nightclub for a VIP experience during your stay.

8. Venetian Theatre At The Venetian Las Vegas

The Venetian is a luxurious place to watch live music and be pampered with all of the finer things in life. Their current shows include Chicago, Joey Yung, Atomic Saloon Show, Foreigner, and Human Nature. The Atomic Saloon show has a specialty cocktail menu that entertains guests with comedy, singing, and aerial stunt routines.

This is one place anyone with cash has to visit due to its popularity and undeniable ability to blow its patrons away. Guests can also visit the nightclubs, high-end restaurants, or stay in a fancy suite while visiting this particular venue.

7. Rose. Rabbit. Lie.

This venue can be found at the Cosmopolitan that gives guests a more intimate and private setting compared to a large concert hall. You will be seated at a table where classic dishes are given a modern twist and live music is the cherry on top.

They do require guests to wear more formal attire to fit the atmosphere of sophistication and the entertainment changes nightly. You will be surrounded by pricey foods that taste out of this world and crystalline glasses filled to the brim with your favorite beverage. It was made for those with deep pockets and will continue to host this group of people due to its increasing popularity.

6. Encore Theater

This theater is located in the Wynn Resort that has everything from live music to entire productions for guests to come and watch. A few of their current performers include artists like Drake, Lionel Richie, and Bryan Adams, but there are also aerial shows and comedians on their list as well.

The great thing about this theater is that once you are done watching your favorite artist perform, you can head over to their XS Nightclub where the fun times with Drake continue or you can spend your money shopping or at the casino.

5. Zappos Theater At Planet Hollywood

This theater is found at the Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino, and it can hold up to 7,000 guests. Those willing to splurge on VIP seats will be given a private table with bottle service to help complete their listening experience.

There are projectors and lights all over the place with LED walls and a superior sound system that is unlike anything else available in Las Vegas. You can purchase a meet and greet with one of the many artists such as Gwen Stefani, Kelly Clarkson, Florida Georgia Line, and Shania Twain.

4. The Colosseum At Caesars Palace

Caesars Palace is well-known on the Las Vegas strip, and part of its fame comes from its live music experience. They have a sound system that will make you move with the music, as it is a more intimate setting with only 4,300 seats.

There is no bad seat, but the VIP area is hard to beat as guests are offered private tables, a bottle and cocktail service, and a private lobby with other drinks and snacks. You can watch artists perform such as Journey, Rod Stewart, Madonna, and Keith Urban. Your bank account might be drained, but the experience you have will make you feel like a queen.

3. Hanks Fine Steaks & Martinis

This is a high-end bar and lounge that also has live music for guests to listen to while they eat their elegant dinners. They serve specialty cocktails and meals that will have you gasping at the bill, although, the music will help to soothe your aching soul.

They have had several artists play such as Michael Grimm, but they often have other artists that can play a wide variety of tunes. The best place to sit is up near the bar if your number one desire is to see the performance, but the steaks they serve might avert your attention.

2. House Of Blues

This venue is a phenomenal place to go if you don't mind spending the extra dough for a front-row seat. You can order VIP tickets and be right up next to the stage, as well as have a meet and greet with the performers.

It provides an intimate experience where you can witness world-class performers while being pampered by the staff. It is found inside Mandalay Bay and their constant flux of artists allows guests to see some of their favorites perform on stage.

1. Pearl Concert Theater At Palms Casino Resort

This venue is great for the rich and wealthy as it has several private bars and lounges for them to frequent throughout the evening. You can order drinks and sit away from the crowds while having the night of your life in this premier location.

A few of their up and coming performers include Styx, Dermot Kennedy, and Billy Idol. It will be an experience you never forget, and your wallet won't either, as you discover the finer things in life money has to offer.
