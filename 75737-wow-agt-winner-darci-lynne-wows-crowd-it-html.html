Wow! 'AGT' winner Darci Lynne wows crowd with Italian opera at a Las Vegas show

June 27, 2024
blog

Ever since winning America’s Got Talent, Darci Lynne Farmer has been surprising audiences. In a performance last year, she sang opera.

The teenager managed to throw her voice as she pretended that her puppet was singing. Farmer even hit all the high notes without moving her lips reported Country Rebel.

Farmer, 13, won season 12 of America's Got Talent and with that was able to be a headlining act in her very own Las Vegas show. For her performances, the ventriloquist brought along her puppet friends.

They included flirty Edna, shy Oscar, and the energetic Petunia. Farmer surprised audience members by preparing a new song for Petunia to sing.

"I'm only 13 years old; I don't know anything about opera. Besides, I don't speak Italian."

After bringing out the cute white bunny, the ventriloquist asked her what she'd like to do next. Petunia responded, "I thought it would be nice to do some Italian opera!"

The teenager looked shocked and told the puppet that she didn’t know any Italian let alone how to sing opera. Petunia responded, "Sure you do! Haven't you ever been to Olive Garden?"

Then the puppet showed off all the Italian she knew thanks to the dishes served at Olive Garden like rigatoni, spaghetti, lasagna, and linguini. This somewhat made Farmer relent and the song began.

After just a few lyrics, the sold-out audience went wild, shocked by the young teenager's talent. She continued to sing the beautiful soprano opera that had her hitting notes without even opening her mouth.

At the same time, she kept a perfect vibrato and managed to move Petunia around as well. The performance was amazing and the crowd loved every minute of it.
