Britt Baker Says More Wrestling Fans Are Becoming Customers At Her Dentist Office

July 10, 2024
blog

. is becoming a widely known household name which has resulted in a dramatic uptick in customer counts to her dentist office. In a recent interview at Planet Comic-Con, Baker said she appreciates that fans are visiting her dentist office for actual teeth cleanings and fillings, not just for autographs and pictures.

Baker said, “The number of customers is rising rapidly. I’m getting a lot more fans. Surprisingly a lot of people are driving from Jacksonville to Orlando because they’ve been in a crowd for our Jacksonville shows. It’s cool though because they’re not there to just chat or take a picture or get an autograph, they’re there for an appointment and to get a cleaning. It’s kind of cool because I really am a role model. We’re improving oral health.”

Britt said she truly believes that fans are more willing to listen to her dental advice because of the amount of respect she has gained. Baker said “I’m really good at picking up on when they’re a fan or when they’re not and I get really excited when it’s a wrestling fan because I know they’re there for two reasons and they’re excited. I’m a wrestling fan too so I put myself in their position. But I think so, I like to think they respect me a little more than the average dentist because I’m the best in the game right now.”

Dr. Britt Baker practices at Woodland Lakes Dental in Orlando, Florida. Her dental website can be seen here.
