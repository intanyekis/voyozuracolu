Stevin Smith – Professional Career

Smith was a two-time All-Pac-10 pick as a point guard at Arizona State University from 1991 to 1994. However, he was also involved in the Arizona State point-shaving incident in 1994, alongside ASU teammate Isaac Burton.

On January 28, 1994, Smith allegedly received $20,000 for shaving points in a game against Oregon State. He was arrested in the summer of 1997, and Smith and Burton pled guilty to conspiracy charges in December 1997, admitting to collecting payments to rig four games in 1994. Smith received a one-year and one-day prison sentence.

Smith holds the Arizona State Records career records for the most three-point attempts attempted and made. He and Fat Lever share the career steals record.
