Who is Mark Neveldine

Mark Neveldine is a film director, producer, and screenwriter from America. He is famous for movies such as Crank, Crank: High Voltage, and Gamer. Furthermore, he also directed the 2012 hit movie Ghost Rider: Spirit of Vengeance.
