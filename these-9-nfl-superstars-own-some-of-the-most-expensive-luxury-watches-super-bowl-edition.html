NFL is a valuable sports league that offers hefty salaries and contracts to players, with an average deal of $860,000. The league also helps the players become popular through brand endorsements and commercials. With an opportunity to earn millions, the players have expensive tastes to match their lifestyles. As the Annual Super Bowl rolls on, the top athletes in the association go head-to-head to take home the Championship trophy. Over the last two years, some of the highest-paid athletes with contracts worth tens of millions of dollars have had the opportunity to play on the field.

From Rolex and Patek Phillipe to Breitling, some NFL stars who play the Super Bowl have the most stunning watches.

Odell Beckham Jr.

The newest addition to the 2022 reigning championships Los Angeles Rams, Odell Beckham Jr., is one of the most stylish players in the NFL with rich tastes. An enthusiast of customized and rare watches, the NFL star is spotted wearing Patek Philippe Nautilus that costs upwards of $30,000, as reported by GQ. He also owns a customized Breitling with a stunning diamond case.

Aaron Donald

LA Rams’ defender Aaron Donald has an eye for Rolex watches. The star has been spotted wearing Rolex President watches in yellow gold, and platinum. The Day-Date President watches have been a popular choice by celebrities as it is one of the most expensive and vintage timepieces introduced by Rolex. Introduced in 1956, it comes with a three-rounded link bracelet. The cost of his Rolex watches is upwards of $110,000.

Joe Burrow

Joe Burrow, the young quarterback of the Cincinnati Bengals, has an icy style when choosing his watches as they come in frosted designs. Wrist Enthusiast mentions that he gets the watches by Leo Frost, a watch customizer who details the timepieces with diamonds. The NFL star owns a Cartier Santos customized in diamonds for the player. For the 2022 Super Bowl, the star entered with a Cartier Santos strapped to his wrist made from black diamonds.

Ja'Marr Chase

Ja'Marr Chase signed one of the highest rookie contracts when the wide receiver made a deal at almost $31 million. Chase is often spotted wearing expensive diamond watches from luxury watchmakers like Rolex with a sprawling net worth of multi-million dollars. He is usually wearing a 16528 GMT Master 16718 and Cosmograph Daytona. The diamond-studded custom watches cost the NFL player upwards of $50,000.

Nick Bosa

A star from the 2020 Super Bowl, Nick Bosa was a smart choice by the San Francisco 49ers to stand strong at their defense. According to CBS Sports, Bosa makes $27 million a year with a five-year contract that will likely expand after the 2022 season. An elegant player in every season, Nick Bosa attended the contract signing conference in style when he was spotted wearing a stainless steel Rolex Submariner, an iconic timepiece worn by many celebrities and dignitaries around the world. The watch retails upwards of $40,000.

Mitchell Schwartz

An Offensive tackle for Kansas City Chiefs, Mitchell Schwartz has an $11.255 million contract under his name. One of the biggest watch collectors on the list, Schwartz is often spotted wearing timeless brands such as Rolex, Vacheron Constantin, and Patek Philippe. Along with a Rolex Sky-Dweller, the NFL star also has a Blancpain Fifty Fathoms and a handcrafted yellow dial F.P. Journe Centigraphe.

Patrick Mahomes I

The star quarterback for Kansas City Chiefs, Patrick Mahomes, has a whopping $500 million, a 10-year contract under his belt to play with the NFL team. With a sprawling net worth that can buy any watch he wants, Mahomes leans more towards Rolex when showing his watch game. He is spotted wearing the Rolex Daytona Rainbow in Everose gold which features a rainbow-colored sapphire bezel and costs $86,000, as reported by HypeBeast. He is also known to own several watches from the Yacht-Master collection by Rolex and the GMT Master II Batman timepiece.

Tom Brady

One of the greatest football players, Tom Brady, announced his retirement from the NFL in February 2022; however, the player has been rocking some stunning timepieces for his seven Super Bowl wins. The NFL legend wears the watches and is also the brand ambassador to advertise them. After his contract ended with Tag Heuer, Brady joined hands with IWC to bring their collection to life. After his iconic win at the 2020 Super Bowl, Brady was spotted wearing an IWC Top Gun. He also owns several Rolex watches, including a yellow gold Rolex Presidential Day-Date.

With the jaw-dropping salaries earned by the NFL stars, it is no surprise that each player has a unique collection that suits his style and persona. While some of the stars own popular timepieces worn by celebrities, some have a collection with rare watches that will become iconic pieces as time goes on.
